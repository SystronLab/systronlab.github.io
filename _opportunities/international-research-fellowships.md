Several prestigious international fellowship opportunities for 2026 have now been announced. These schemes support early career and postdoctoral researchers aiming to develop independent research careers through international mobility and collaboration with UK host institutions.

## British Academy – International Fellowships

This scheme supports outstanding early career researchers in taking their first steps towards an independent research career by providing international research experience in the UK. Fellows are hosted at a UK institution and benefit from mentorship, research funding, and structured career development.

The fellowship is particularly suited to researchers in the humanities and social sciences seeking to establish long term international collaborations and strengthen their academic profile.

More details: [https://www.thebritishacademy.ac.uk/funding/schemes/international-fellowships/](https://www.thebritishacademy.ac.uk/funding/schemes/international-fellowships/)

## Royal Society – Newton International Fellowships

The Newton International Fellowship is aimed at early career researchers based outside the UK who wish to undertake high quality research at a UK host institution. The scheme enables researchers to build sustainable research links and integrate into the UK research environment.

The fellowship typically includes a competitive living allowance, research expenses, and support for professional development over a two year period.

More details: [https://royalsociety.org/grants/newton-international/](https://royalsociety.org/grants/newton-international/)

## MSCA Postdoctoral Fellowships 2026

Marie Skłodowska Curie Actions Postdoctoral Fellowships are fully funded fellowships open to researchers of all nationalities. They support advanced training, international mobility, and career development through high impact research.

The scheme supports mobility within Europe, into Europe, and to the UK, and is open to researchers across all disciplines.

More details: [https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/calls/msca-postdoctoral-fellowships-2026](https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/calls/msca-postdoctoral-fellowships-2026)
