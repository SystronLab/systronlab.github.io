The SYSTRON LAB was delighted to exhibit at the UK’s flagship cyber security event during its 10th anniversary year.

Together with our CHEDDAR Hub partners from Imperial College London and the University of Glasgow, we engaged with researchers, industry leaders, government stakeholders, and cyber security practitioners to discuss the future of resilient and secure communication networks.

![CyberUK-4](/img/posts/systron-cyberuk4-2026.jpg)

![CyberUK-1](/img/posts/systron-cyberuk1-2026.jpg)

At our stand, we showcased ongoing research exploring how next-generation wireless systems, Open RAN platforms and resilient network infrastructures, can support critical applications and strengthen cyber defence capabilities.
