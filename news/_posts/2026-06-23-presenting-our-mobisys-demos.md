We have recently demonstrated some of our ongoing research at [the 24th ACM International Conference on Mobile Systems, Applications, and Services (ACM MobiSys 2026)](https://www.sigmobile.org/mobisys/2026/), hosted in Cambridge between the 21st and 26th June.
Our demonstrations, covering both the interactive network connectivity mapping of mobile network operators, in addition to work to mitigate radio resource exhaustion risks in private 5G networks, both represent notable contributions to the research (and industrial) telecommunications communities. 

![Poonam presents 5G work](/img/posts/mobisys-demo-poonam-shows.jpg)

It was exciting to have such engaging conversations with so many of the attendees, finding potential opportunities for future collaboration -- both in research and in potential future projects -- and contribute to the mobile systems community with so many people representing different universities and communities from across the world.
