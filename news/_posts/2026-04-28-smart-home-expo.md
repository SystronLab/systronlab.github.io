Speak with Dr Poonam Yadav, part of the [Connectivity Standards Alliance (CSA)](https://csa-iot.org/) and board advisor to [Microelectronics UK](https://microelectronicsuk.com), as she attends the [Smart Home Expo](https://smarthomeexpo.in.messefrankfurt.com/mumbai/en.html) in Mumbai, India, at the end of April this year.

Returning for its 7th edition from the 28th-30th April, the three-day event is hosted at the Jio World Convention Centre and unites over 450 leading brands demonstrating the latest in smart home technology - a key focus of [some of the IoT research performed by our lab](/projects/iot).

If you're attending, please connect with Poonam - you'll see her speaking at a selection of panel sessions, and exploring the event. You can also [reach out to her through LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/pyadav/) or drop her an email at [poonam.yadav@york.ac.uk](mailto:poonam.yadav@york.ac.uk) to arrange a time to catch up.
