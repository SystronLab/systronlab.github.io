Current research focuses on the security of next-generation mobile networks, with a particular emphasis on O-RAN (Open Radio Access Network) architectures and 6G security. This work investigates threats, vulnerabilities, and defensive mechanisms in open and disaggregated RAN environments, contributing to building more secure and trustworthy future wireless infrastructure.
